About Russell Technical Writing

Hello,
My name is Karen Russell, I am the director and technical writer at Russell Technical Writing Ltd.

I'm an enthusiastic, forward-thinking, and reliable professional with vast experience communicating complex scientific ideas and technical information - for almost ten years as a scientific technical author / information developer, and seven years as a chemistry teacher.

Open to taking on technical writing projects as a freelancer or contractor.

From a technical writing perspective, I'm well experienced in utilising structured authoring tools such as Oxygen and XMetal, and organising modular DITA topics and maps within a CCMS.

My particular strengths are:

  • recognising and resolving gaps in documentation and removing redundant or duplicate content
  • restructuring complex or confusing documentation into clear, concise, and coherent documentation with a user-friendly layout

Aside from the technical writing, I'm familiar with working in an Agile environment using Atlassian Confluence and JIRA. My naturally creative streak complimented by my tendency to “think outside the box” contribute to my strong problem solving capability and confident composure.

I am also a strong advocate of improving customer / user experience.

After listening to your requirements, I can provide a personalised approach to meet your documentation needs, regardless of the size of your company, or complexity of your products.

Why Hire Russell Technical Writing Ltd?

Some of the scenarios in which we can we can help your business improve customer experience.

  • Has your legacy documentation become a "dumping ground"?
    Do you have several authors/developers adding content without addressing duplication or redundant information?

    Save time and effort. Let your developers do what they do best.
    I can analyse and re-organise your documentation in a concise and logical format (while consulting your subject matterexperts as required).

  • Are you preparing documentation for translation?
    Do you want to save translation costs by reusing content?

    Send only the new and edited modular content for translation. Translate once, use repeatedly. DITA structure also promotes a consistent and easy-to-navigate layout which enhances user-experience.
    I have experienced migrating vast quantities of documentation from unstructured writing to structured DITA format. I can confidently assist you (at any point in your DITA journey), with over 6 years of experience writing in DITA format.

  • Do you need a helping hand to create internal or customer-facing documentation?

    Free-up valuable staff resources. Avoid hiring another permanent staff member.
    I can work autonomously or dynamically within any team to get the job done.

  • Would you like documentation to include clear and informative diagrams?

    I can provide informative diagrams to compliment well-structured text.
    If required, I can also assist with designing company logos.
